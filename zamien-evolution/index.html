How To Play

To starting playing the online version of RPS: City Edition v. 2.0.1, choose which cities/boroughs/states/ you want to play as your options.

Your choices will replace the standard "Rock", "Paper", and "Scissors" options.

Once you selected which locations you are using, then enter your initials input box with "Enter Initials here".

To begin playing, all you have to do is select your location and then the screen will tell you if your 'move' beats the cpu's 'move'.

Your wins and losses will be recored below the buttons for you to track your score.

When you are done playing hit the save button to record your score in the leaderboard.
